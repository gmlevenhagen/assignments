How To Make French Press Coffee

By Garett Levenhagen

Step 1

First, Boil water and make sure to have the French Press ready with a bag of ground coffee to scoop from.

Step 2

Second, Scoop 5 tablespoons of ground coffee into the French Press.

Step 3

Third, Pour the boiling water into the french press and let steep for at least 4 minutes.

Step 4

Four, Press the coffee to push the grounds to the bottom of the French Press.

Step 5

Finally, pour into a mug.

Now enjoy the coffee!
